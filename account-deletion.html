Account Deletion – Prumo

You can delete your Prumo account at any time, directly from within the app. No need to contact support.

How to Delete Your Account

Follow these steps inside the Prumo app to permanently delete your account:

  1. Open the Prumo app on your Android device.
  2. Tap on Profile in the bottom navigation bar.
  3. Scroll down and tap Settings.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Read the confirmation message and tap Confirm Delete to proceed.

Your account deletion will be processed immediately upon confirmation.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently deleted:

  • Your user profile (name, email address, and preferences)
  • All content you have created or uploaded within the app
  • Your authentication credentials
  • Any personal settings associated with your account

Data Retention

Certain data may be retained after account deletion for legal or financial compliance purposes. Specifically, transaction records (if applicable) may be retained for the period required by applicable law. This data is kept solely to meet legal obligations and is not used for any other purpose.

Retained data is securely stored and will be deleted as soon as the legally required retention period expires.

Deletion Timeframe

Your account and personal data are deleted immediately when you confirm the deletion request in the app.

Any copies that may exist in encrypted backup systems are fully purged within 30 days.

Privacy & Infrastructure

Prumo uses Supabase as its backend infrastructure provider. Supabase processes and stores user data on behalf of Prumo in accordance with their Privacy Policy. Account deletion requests are forwarded to Supabase, which removes the associated data from its systems within the timeframe described above.
